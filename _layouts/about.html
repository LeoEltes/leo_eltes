I study Music Production and Songwritng at Örebro's School of Music in Sweden. My love for exploring and experimenting intermedial expressions with music comes from an early appreciation of film scores. The experience of audio combined with visuals is particularly important to me and I constantly seek to be a part of composing sounds that resonates with the emotions of the visuals.


Most of my inspiration regarding sound design comes from minimalistic synth producers and artists such as Trent Reznor (from Nine Inch Nails), Nils Frahm, Mike Lindsay, James Blake, Bon Iver and Grouper. But I take great melodic and emotional inspiration from artists with pioneering voices like Lorde, Joni Mitchell, Arcade Fire and Björk. With these contemporaries in mind my music usually takes a minimalistic approach, where less is more. Letting the observer or listener process and decide what is of importance in the art.


Alongside the love for music and film, I have a passion for programming and exploring the combination and expressiveness of programming and sound put together. I've programmed visuals to some of the tracks from my debut EP, Bedroom Productions (you may watch these by clicking on Visuals in the menu bar). Some of these visuals are interactive and dependant on the user's action as well as the frequencies the song journeys through.


Developing and communicating ideas are a big part of writing and producing music, whether it is for film or for artists. As a participant in SR's (Sveriges Radio, the public radio of Sweden) Creative Lab 2018, as well as a composer for various video documentary projects, I have explored and practiced, evaluating and discussing ideas from different angles.


I'm also unquestionably professional at taking care of and recording cats.
