Barber Shops and Pizza Places

Kungsbacka is a moderately large town on the west coast of Sweden with all public transport originating from the central station. Watch as the different vehicles travel through a canvas without a map, creating music as they go.

Please start with a low volume, the audio may clip and stutter depending on your web browser. Browsers on which the project is currently working properly are Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome

This project is not ready for mobile yet, please visit on a desktop browser

What's going on? Data is being retreived from Västtrafik using their public API. The data contains information about the vehicles' coordinates in real time. Each circle represents a vehicle and its corresponding location. Depending on its location a tone is produced.

Sometimes you may hear a slight echo, it means that the vehicle is delayed and will not arrive on schedule. The stereo field also changes depending on where the vehicle is located. Listen and try to figure out what data all of the sounds are controlled by.

The glitches and bugs that appear are sadly on Västtrafik's end and I am not skilled (or patient) enough to work around it. See it as an artistic flavour!
